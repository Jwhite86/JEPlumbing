J&EPlumbing can provide quality tubing connectors and pipe fittings to help you set up your fluid handling system with no leaks, flow restrictions or costly downtime. J&EPlumbing tubing connector range includes barbed fittings for flexible tubing, compression fittings or pipe connectors for rigid tubing. Tubing connectors and pipe fittings are available in sizing to connect anything from microbore tubing up to pipes. We offer a variety of materials such as low cost polypropylene (PP) for general applications to PTFE for corrosive media, formulations that comply with USP regulations, are FDA compliant; and for no extra charge we can offer certificates of fittings compliance for our range in tubing connectors and pipe fittings. J&EPlumbing provides you with the information you need to select an optimal fitting. We provide the basic physical property information including temperature and pressure rating for each material formulation. Our chemical resistance charts help determine the fitting material with best chemical compatibility to your process.
